The agency

We don't rent boats.
We craft the voyage.

Founded in 2013 by Fresa and Marc, Flores Runaway was one of the first to open up Komodo by sea — taking travellers on unforgettable journeys by boat and by land ever since.

Tell us how you like to travel and we'll match you to the right boat, design the route, and handle every detail — from sunrise at Padar to the manta rays at Karang Makassar. Our mission is simple: to design the journey of a lifetime for you.

One contactFrom enquiry to last anchor. A single advisor who knows your trip inside out.
Local crewsBorn to the Flores Sea. Captains and guides from Flores and Sulawesi, never flown in for the season.
BespokeBuilt around you. Diving, honeymoon, family or expedition — every itinerary is designed, not booked.
